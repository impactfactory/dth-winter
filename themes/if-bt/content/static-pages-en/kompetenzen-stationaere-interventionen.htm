As a specialist in interventional cardiology and pioneer in catheter-based heart valve replacement therapy, Dr Olaf Franzen offers catheter-based interventions at the Hirslanden Klinik im Park in Zurich. Dr Franzen has many years of international expertise in Mitraclip, TAVI, PTCA interventions, LAA atrial appendage closure and closure of septal defects. The catheter-based interventions are all minimally invasive. This is particularly valuable for patients who cannot or do not want open-heart surgery and prefer a shorter hospital stay.

  • Aortic valve implantation (TAVI)
  • Mitre valve repair (Mitraclip)
  • Coronary angioplasty (PTCA/PCI)
  • Atrial appendage closure (LAA)
  • Closure of septal defects (ASD / VSD / PFO)
  • Electrical cardioversion
  • Closure of paravalvular leaks
